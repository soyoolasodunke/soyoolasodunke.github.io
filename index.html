About Me

Soyoola Sodunke is a dynamic Presales Engineer, whose career spans over a decade of driving innovation, operational excellence, and strategic transformation across telecommunications and technology sectors. With a foundation built on a passion for solving complex business problems through data, he brings a rare blend of technical depth, business acumen, and client empathy to every engagement.

His journey includes pivotal roles at Airtel Nigeria and Tech Mahindra, where he championed KYC optimization, churn management, and enterprise provisioning, contributing directly to daily profit margins and customer retention.

Soyoola is certified in Databricks Lakehouse and Cloud Computing, and fluent in technologies like SQL, Spark, Excel, and Power BI. His soft skills—strategic thinking, client relationship management, and effective communication—make him not just a solution architect, but a trusted advisor.

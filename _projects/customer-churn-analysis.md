## Overview

## Key Insights

- Customers on month-to-month contracts churned more often.
- Short-tenure customers showed higher risk.
- Certain service bundles were associated with lower churn.

## Business Recommendation

Focus retention interventions on high-risk segments and redesign onboarding for early-tenure customers.

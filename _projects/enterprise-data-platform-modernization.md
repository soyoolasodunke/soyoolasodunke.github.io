## Client Context
Large financial services organization with fragmented legacy data systems.

## Business Problem
- Slow reporting cycles (days → weeks)
- Inconsistent data across departments
- Limited ability to support advanced analytics

## Technical Challenge
- Multiple on-prem data silos
- Poor data governance
- Lack of scalable infrastructure

## My Role
- Led technical discovery sessions with IT and business stakeholders
- Translated business reporting challenges into data architecture requirements
- Partnered with Account Executive to position a modern cloud data platform

## Solution
- Designed a cloud-native data platform (Lakehouse + warehouse architecture)
- Introduced centralized data governance and ingestion pipelines
- Proposed scalable compute for analytics workloads

## Presales Activities
- Ran discovery workshops across finance and operations teams
- Delivered tailored architecture walkthroughs
- Developed a tailored technical proposal
- Built a demo showcasing real-time reporting capabilities
- Ensured a smooth project transition to the delivery team

## Outcome
- Reduced reporting time from days to hours
- Enabled foundation for AI/ML initiatives
- Contributed to successful deal closure within 8-week cycle

## Key Takeaways
Aligning technical architecture with reporting pain points accelerated stakeholder buy-in.

## Tools & Technologies
Azure Stack, Databricks, Delta Lake, Spark, Azure ML, Microsoft Purview, Entra ID, etc

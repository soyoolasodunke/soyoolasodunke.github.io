## Client Context
Telecommunications provider facing increasing customer churn.

## Business Problem
- High churn impacting revenue
- Limited visibility into customer behavior drivers

## Technical Challenge
- Disparate customer data sources
- Lack of predictive analytics capability

## My Role
- Identified churn as a high-impact AI use case during discovery
- Mapped ML capabilities to revenue retention objectives
- Positioned solution in business terms for executive stakeholders

## Solution
- Designed predictive churn model using customer behavior data
- Integrated model outputs into business dashboards
- Enabled proactive customer retention strategies

## Presales Activities
- Conducted value-focused discovery sessions
- Delivered demo of churn prediction dashboard
- Built ROI narrative linking churn reduction to revenue impact

## Outcome
- Identified high-risk customers with ~85% accuracy
- Enabled targeted retention campaigns
- Strengthened business case for AI investment

## Key Takeaways
Framing AI in terms of revenue protection significantly improved executive engagement.

## Tools & Technologies
Python, ZenML, Scikit-learn, Power BI, SQL

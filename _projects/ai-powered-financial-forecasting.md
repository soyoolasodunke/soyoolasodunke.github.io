## Client Context
Mid-sized enterprise seeking to improve financial planning accuracy.

## Business Problem
- Inaccurate forecasts affecting budgeting decisions
- Manual and time-consuming forecasting processes

## Technical Challenge
- Limited historical data quality
- Lack of advanced modeling techniques

## My Role
- Identified forecasting as a strategic AI opportunity
- Translated finance team needs into model requirements
- Positioned solution to CFO-level stakeholders

## Solution
- Built machine learning forecasting models
- Integrated outputs into financial planning workflows
- Automated data preparation and model updates

## Presales Activities
- Conducted executive-level presentations
- Developed ROI model showing cost savings and accuracy gains
- Delivered proof-of-concept with sample data

## Outcome
- Improved forecast accuracy by ~20%
- Reduced manual effort in financial planning
- Strengthened executive confidence in AI adoption

## Key Takeaways
Executive alignment is critical when positioning AI in finance functions.

## Tools & Technologies
Python, Pandas, Prophet, Excel, Power BI

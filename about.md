# About Me

I am a **Presales Engineer** with a strong interest in **data solutions, analytics, and business-focused technology advisory**. My work sits at the intersection of business needs and technical execution. I enjoy helping organizations understand their challenges, shape the right solution approach, and communicate value clearly to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

My professional focus is on translating complex requirements into practical and well-structured solution ideas. This includes understanding customer pain points, supporting solution discovery, explaining technical concepts in a simple way, and aligning technology recommendations with business goals.

## My Professional Approach

I believe good solution engineering is not just about knowing the technology. It is about asking the right questions, identifying the real business problem, and presenting a solution in a way that is practical, credible, and easy to understand.

My approach is guided by a few principles:

### 1. Business First
Technology should solve a real problem. I always try to understand the business context before jumping into tools or architecture.

### 2. Clarity Matters
A strong solution loses impact if it is poorly explained. I value simple, structured, and professional communication.

### 3. Practical Over Theoretical
I appreciate solutions that can realistically be implemented, maintained, and adopted by the customer.

### 4. Insight Creates Value
Data is only useful when it helps people make better decisions. I am particularly interested in how analytics can improve visibility, strategy, and performance.
